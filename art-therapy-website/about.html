What is Art Therapy?

Art therapy is a mental health profession using the creative process to improve physical, mental, and emotional well-being. It’s especially useful when words are insufficient.

What is Meditation?

Meditation is the practice of training attention to cultivate present-moment awareness and calm the mind.

Therapeutic Benefits

  • Emotional regulation
    Helps process and release difficult emotions.
  • Reduced stress
    Shown to lower cortisol and support relaxation.
  • Increased self-awareness
    Explore feelings without judgment.
  • Boosts self-esteem
    Creative achievement fuels confidence.
  • Trauma processing
    Nonverbal exploration while nervous system is regulated.
