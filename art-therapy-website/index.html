What is Art Therapy?

Art therapy uses the creative process to improve physical, mental, and emotional well-being — focusing on self-expression, not artistic skill.

What is Meditation?

Meditation trains the mind for present-moment awareness, reducing stress and improving focus — ideal to pair with art therapy.

Mandala Technique

A mandala is a circular pattern used to center attention and promote self-soothing through focused creative expression.

Quick Session Structure

  1. Mindful check-in (short guided meditation)
  2. Guided art activity (e.g., Draw Your Emotion)
  3. Mindful reflection and integration
